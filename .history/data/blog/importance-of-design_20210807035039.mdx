Let me start out by saying what is probably the most important point in a clear enough way that its easily appreciated,

> It is not minimalism when you are simply choosing not to work on the appearance of a site.

Which may seem harsh, but far too often sites and web applications are branded as minimalist when in reality minimal yet functional design was not a consideration in making the site, merely minimal effort put into the site's appearance outside of questionable choices in regards to topography.
